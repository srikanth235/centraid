# Centraid Companion privacy policy

Centraid Companion sends data only to the Centraid gateway the owner pairs. Centraid does not operate an analytics, advertising, or extension telemetry service.

The extension may process a page origin and detected sign-in field kinds to find matching Locker items. Only secret-free item labels are considered before the owner chooses one. That click requests one password reveal and, when configured, a derived TOTP code; the OTP seed remains in the vault. The reveal receipt records the normalized page origin. Fill material is not written to extension storage.

When the owner chooses a capture action, the current title, URL, selected text, or visible screenshot is sent to the chosen Centraid app. Nothing is captured merely by browsing. The paired gateway may use an encrypted iroh relay; a relay can observe transport metadata but cannot read tunnel contents.

The owner can delete the local device identity with Unpair and can revoke it remotely in Centraid Settings → Devices. A remote revocation invalidates the next gateway request and triggers local pairing-state removal.
