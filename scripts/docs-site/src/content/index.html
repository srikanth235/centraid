Centraid · the manual

Your data. Your apps. Your devices.

Centraid is personal software with the ownership inverted. One vault on your own machine holds the canonical record of your life on a single shared ontology — no silos. Apps are thin projections over it, built and rebuilt by an agent to fit you. Devices are clients of one gateway you run, connected peer-to-peer.

Two ways in. Pick the one you need right now.

path 1 · use it

I want it running today.

Install from source, open the desktop shell, get a vault, pair your phone, clone your first app, and let an automation pull your first external data in. No theory — a straight line from git clone to a living vault.
