The deep end

The ontology has its own chapter.

The claim that Centraid has no data silos rests on one artifact: a single person-owned data model where identity, time, money, content, activity, observation and relationships are declared exactly once, and every app either extends or references them — never re-declares them. It is rendered as a live, explorable document: the full schema, the entity map, the ownership matrix, the gateway contract, and the ten rules that keep it honest.
